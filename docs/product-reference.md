## What We Offer

Private chauffeured European tours for 1–19 guests. Your own vehicle, your own driver, your own pace. No strangers on the bus. No fixed schedule. Just your family (or friends, or group) exploring Europe the way it should be explored — privately, comfortably, and without a single logistical worry.

## The Experience

### How It Works (Consumer Version)
1. **Tell us about your trip** — Who's traveling, when, what you'd love to see. WhatsApp us or fill in the form.
2. **We design your journey** — Day-by-day itinerary matched to your pace, interests, and budget. Hotels handpicked. Vehicle selected for your group size.
3. **You travel** — Your chauffeur meets you at the airport. Itinerary in hand. Hotels confirmed. Every detail handled. You just enjoy.
4. **We're always there** — 24/7 support throughout your trip. Real humans who know Europe, not a call centre.

### What's Included (Typical Programme)
- Private chauffeured vehicle throughout (Mercedes fleet)
- 4-star boutique hotel accommodation
- Daily breakfast
- Airport arrival & departure transfers
- English-speaking driver with deep local knowledge
- 24/7 on-trip support
- Customizable day-by-day itinerary

### What's Not Included (Typical Programme)
- International flights
- Travel insurance
- Lunches and dinners (recommendations provided)
- Entrance fees to attractions
- Tips and gratuities
- Personal expenses
- Visa processing
